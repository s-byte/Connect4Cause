WHY HELP UT?
IT'S NONPROFIT:
We are a nonprofit organization, which means that we do not collect money to run our business. Our motivation does not come from monetary rewards, but rather our intentions to do good in the world. This way, when you decide to use our services and resources to help out, you will be guaranteed to be doing good for any of the organizations shown.
IT'S AT YOUR LEISURE:
By using our opportunities, you wouldn’t need to pre-plan far ahead of time to help out. The websites that can be found on our map contain flexible events that are easily accessible. While you are relaxing at the beach or enjoying your hike down a forest path one day, you can contribute to making the world a better place the next!
THE LITTLE THINGS ADD UP:
Even if you do not think that one person will make a difference, the truth is that it does. When you decide to take a few days from your vacation to help other people native to the country, you will create a chain of events that started from you! One action will always go a long way even if it doesn’t seem like it in the beginning.
HELPING IS TYPICALLY FREE:
When you book a volunteering trip outside of the country, you would normally have to pay for everything. However, with our organization you can simply look for opportunities to help out wherever you decide to travel to! This way, you don’t have to pay for another trip but rather, kill two birds with one stone. Who doesn’t love free stuff?
